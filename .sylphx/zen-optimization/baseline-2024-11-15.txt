ANALYSIS
========

Strengths:
- Signal creation is competitive (1.25x gap)
- Lightweight bundle (3.75 KB)

Critical Issues:
1. Fanout performance (25.3x gap) - URGENT

3. Computed creation (5x gap)

Gap Breakdown Hypothesis:
